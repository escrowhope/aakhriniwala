How ordering works

Everything starts and ends on WhatsApp — no apps, no accounts, no waiting on hold. Here's exactly what happens, depending on what you're ordering.

Fresh & Local

1. Fresh food, delivered across Lahore

For anything cooked hot — karahi, nihari, biryani, kebabs, fresh parathas, brownies and cakes — we deliver same-day, anywhere in Lahore.

How to order: Browse the menu, tap any “Order via WhatsApp” button, and your message arrives with us pre-filled. Tell us your order, area and time, and we confirm in minutes.

Payment: Cash on Delivery, or bank transfer / Easypaisa / JazzCash if you prefer to pay ahead. Your choice, no pressure.

Preparation time: Most fresh orders are cooked and out the door within 60–90 minutes of confirmation. Slow-cooked dishes like nihari and paya are made in batches daily — we'll tell you the next available window when you message.

Delivery timeframe: Typically 30–75 minutes after your food is ready, depending on your area. We're open 24/7, so late-night and emergency orders are genuinely welcome.

Packaging: Heat-sealed, leak-proof containers inside an insulated bag so your food arrives hot and intact — never a soggy shopping bag.

Nationwide

2. Shelf-stable orders, shipped across Pakistan

Our achar, chutneys, preserves, frozen parathas and packed baked gift boxes travel the whole country — Karachi, Islamabad, Multan, Peshawar and everywhere between.

How to order: Same WhatsApp flow. Send us the items and your full delivery address with city.

Payment: Cash on Delivery is available nationwide. For orders being sent as gifts, advance bank transfer is simplest so the recipient isn't asked to pay.

Preparation time: Jars and packed boxes are dispatched within 24–48 hours. Frozen items ship in insulated packaging at the start of the week to avoid weekend transit delays.

Delivery timeframe: 2–4 working days via TCS or Leopards, with a tracking number sent to you on WhatsApp the moment it ships.

Packaging: Glass jars are bubble-wrapped and boxed to survive courier handling; baked boxes are sealed and cushioned. If anything ever arrives damaged, send us a photo and we'll make it right.

Made To Order

3. Custom cakes — Lahore, by advance booking

Layer cakes, themed birthday cakes and celebration cakes are crafted to your brief and delivered within Lahore only, so they arrive flawless.

How to order: Message us on WhatsApp with the occasion, flavour, number of people, and any reference photos. We'll send back a design suggestion and a quote.

Payment: A 50% advance via bank transfer confirms your booking and date; the balance is Cash on Delivery or transfer on the day.

Preparation time: Please book at least 48–72 hours ahead. For multi-tier or heavily decorated cakes, 4–5 days gives the best result. Eid and wedding-season dates fill fast — earlier is safer.

Delivery timeframe: Scheduled to your event, with a delivery window agreed in advance. We handle the cake from our kitchen to your door so it never changes hands at the wrong moment.

Packaging: Sturdy cake boxes with internal support for tiered designs, delivered upright in a temperature-aware vehicle.
