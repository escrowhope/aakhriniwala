About this box

The hook. If you only order one thing from us, make it this. It's the box we're known for, and the one people quietly buy again for themselves after gifting it to someone else.

What's inside. Real dark chocolate and cocoa, butter, eggs, sugar, flour, a whisper of espresso to deepen the chocolate, and salt. That's it. No preservatives, no box mix, no shortcuts — because the recipe doesn't need them.

Made-fresh promise. Nothing is pre-baked and waiting. The moment your order is confirmed on WhatsApp, we bake your box. That's why it arrives smelling like an oven, not a packet.

The gifting angle. It arrives in a sealed kraft box stamped with our mark, tied and ready to hand over. Tell us it's a gift and we'll add a handwritten note at no charge — birthdays, apologies, "just because," or a brand-new baby in the family.

Storage. Best within 48 hours at room temperature in the sealed box. Keeps up to 5 days refrigerated — warm for 10 seconds to bring the centre back to molten.
